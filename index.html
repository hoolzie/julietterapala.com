Reaction Radio

Spotify’s API provides a multitude of information about their library of songs, including measures of audio features, such as song valence (or as we like to call it, "positivity") and song energy. These features can correlate with mood. A song high on the valence and energy scales is going to be happy!
Reaction Music App allows you create a playlist of songs, see how they fall on a mood chart, and then lets you edit and sort by mood. Once you have a playlist that you are satisfied with, you can easily export it to your Spotify account.

Reel It

Inspired by community-built databases informed by user-generated content, Reel It provides movie info that extends beyond the credits and critics. Reel It allows users to search movie titles to render robust details including plot, cast/crew, FCC rating, release date, trailers, video reviews, audience ratings, and direct links to purchase, rent, or stream movies. This app combines multiple APIs on a clean, easy to navigate interface. Firebase is used to store and revisit recent searches.

Brew One Two

Designed for beer fans traveling to the Windy City, Brew One Two makes it easy to find a local brew that will match your taste profile. Simply enter your favorite beer brand and maker, and we will connect you with a Chicago-based beverage that you will enjoy. This app utilizes the Brewery DB API, along with a custom built database of Chicago beers, in one easy-to-use web application.
