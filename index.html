Music.I am head of ticketing at the largest independent concert producer in the United States. Over the past ten years I have worked with 1,400+ artists, helping produce 3,500+ shows in 200+ venues across 100+ cities. I have put shows on sale with artists such as Adele, Bob Dylan, Bruce Springsteen, Foo Fighters, Lady Gaga, Mumford & Sons, Muse, and Tom Petty.
I am a bit of a guru when it comes to ticketing security. Over the years I have employed many different strategies that have helped tickets get into the hands of fans and not scalpers.
Need some ticketing help with your artist? Shoot me an email. I’d love to chat.

Web.I design and develop websites. I spend a lot of time studying designs, playing around with code, and learning new technologies. I love to build clean designs with beautiful code that is organized and easily accessible by other coders. I am currently studying at Northwestern University for a full-stack web development certificate. Looking for help with a website? Email me or head over to my development page at juliettedev.com.

Life.I live on the north side of Chicago. I love traveling the world, reading every chance I get, balancing tough yoga poses, and lifting heavy things at the gym.
I try my hand at mixed-media art and photography. I post some of my work on my artist site. Check it out.
I studied Sociology at Northwestern University. During my senior year I conducted a study and wrote a thesis about the hardships of women working in the concert stagehand industry. I received award for it. When I wasn’t studying, I was working on student-produced theatre, either by designing the lighting or by bringing lighting designs to the stage. I find this to be in the same vein as web design and development. I use designs to help communicate the creator’s story, and I make sure the technology behind it works perfectly and seamlessly.
